Manila Ocean Park

The park offers a wide range of activities and entertainment options for visitors of all ages, making it an ideal destination for families with kids. From animal shows to interactive exhibitions, there is something for everyone at Manila Ocean Park. Many love the live action sea lion show and all star bird show. The main attraction of Manila Ocean Park is the Oceanarium which houses 14,000 sea creatures from about 277 species all of which is indigenous in Southeast Asia.
